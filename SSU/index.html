Song Set Help To create a song set:
  1. Tap menu button at upper left.
  2. Enter desired song numbers in the box.
    (no numbers less than 1 or greater than )
  3. Put a comma (,) dot (.) or space ( ) between each song number.
  4. Press "OK"
Example: 6,4,11,26,22,31  (default)

Tip: Lyric sheets for "Singles" or "Sets" may be printed directly from the browser.
